ABOUT QIS CET

Founded in 2004, QIS College of Engineering and Technology is a premier technical institution in Ongole, Prakasam District, Andhra Pradesh.

Approved by AICTE and affiliated to JNTUK, QIS has built an outstanding reputation for academic excellence, industry integration, and holistic student development over two decades.

Our sprawling 30-acre campus houses 30+ state-of-the-art laboratories, a central library with 50,000+ volumes, smart classrooms, sports facilities, and hostels — everything a future engineer needs to thrive.
